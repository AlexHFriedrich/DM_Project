Yeast - moreno_propro 
Undirected, unweighted network of protein-protein interactions in yeast. Nodes represent proteins and edges represent metabolic interaction between the two proteins. 
The network contains 1870 proteins and 2277 edges. 

Adolescent health - moreno_health 
This directed network represents friends among students. Nodes represent students and an edge from a student to another shows that the left student chose the right student as a friend. Edges are weighted, representing interaction strength. 
The network contains 2539 nodes and 12969 edges.
